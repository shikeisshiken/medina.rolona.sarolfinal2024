About

This website will focus on the artists who want to Display their Work of Art. This Website specifically the unrecognized artist from around the world. The artist will be given the option of whether to include their personal information or only their pseudonym. This website will serve as the artists’ online art gallery. Moreover, by developing the website's functionality it generates and serves as an own art gallery of certain artists who desired to have wide recognition in terms of popularity and commission purposes. On the other hand, there will also be a limit of information that the artist will give about themselves. They can only give more information about them by putting a link beside their work that will lead the viewers to their social acc or other media platform that talks about the artist. Art that speaks about intercourse is prohibited as young viewers will also be present in this kind of project. Maintaining kid friendliness is a must.
